# 🎯 Goal Interaction Guide

## How to Interact with Your Goals

### 📱 **Accessing Goals**
1. **From Dashboard**: Click on any goal card in the "Upcoming Goals" or "Recent Activity" sections
2. **From Goal Workspace**: Create new goals and they'll appear in your dashboard
3. **From Progress Visuals**: View your goals in the progress section

### 🚀 **Starting a Goal** (+20 Points)
1. Click on a goal from your dashboard
2. Click the **"Start Goal (+20 pts)"** button
3. ✅ Goal status changes to "IN PROGRESS"
4. 🎉 You earn **20 points** immediately
5. 🔥 Your daily streak counter increases

### 📈 **Updating Progress** (+10 Points per Milestone)
1. In the goal detail screen, use the progress buttons: **25%**, **50%**, **75%**, **100%**
2. Each milestone awards **10 bonus points**:
   - 25% Complete: +10 points
   - 50% Complete: +10 points  
   - 75% Complete: +10 points
3. 🔥 Each progress update counts toward your daily streak

### 🏆 **Completing a Goal** (+100 Points)
1. Click **"Complete Goal (+100 pts)"** button
2. ✅ Goal status changes to "COMPLETED"
3. 📊 Progress automatically set to 100%
4. 🎉 You earn **100 points** for completion
5. 🔥 Completion counts toward your daily streak
6. 🎊 Celebration alerts and notifications

## 🔥 **Streak System**

### **How Streaks Work**
- **Daily Activity**: Any goal interaction (start, progress, complete) counts
- **Streak Counter**: Shows consecutive days with activity
- **Today's Activity**: Shows if you've been active today
- **Automatic Tracking**: No manual input needed

### **Streak Milestones & Rewards**
- **3 Days**: First milestone alert
- **7 Days**: +50 bonus points 🎉
- **14 Days**: Milestone celebration
- **30 Days**: +100 bonus points 🏆
- **60 Days**: Advanced milestone
- **100 Days**: +200 bonus points 🌟
- **365 Days**: +500 bonus points 👑

## 💡 **Pro Tips**

1. **Daily Consistency**: Interact with goals daily to maintain streaks
2. **Small Progress**: Even small updates count toward streaks
3. **Milestone Strategy**: Aim for 25% increments to maximize bonus points
4. **Goal Variety**: Mix different types of goals for balanced development
5. **Regular Check-ins**: Visit the dashboard daily to stay motivated

## 🚨 **Important Notes**
